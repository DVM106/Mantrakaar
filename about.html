Who We Are

Mantrakaar is an independent and forward-thinking digital agency. We specialize in bespoke UI/UX design, high-fidelity web development, conversion-centric search engine optimization (SEO), and data-driven consultancy services.

Founded by Parag Gogari, who brings over 23 years of industry-leading experience, Mantrakaar was established to bridge the gap between complex digital architectures and user-first brand experiences. We don't just build websites; we design digital products that solve real business problems and help companies connect meaningfully with their audience.

With a global footprint spanning operational hubs in both India and the United Kingdom, we collaborate with businesses across healthcare, real estate, manufacturing, hospitality, and retail. Our philosophy is rooted in structural transparency, design aesthetics, and technical excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Mantrakaar focus on custom development over pre-built templates?

Custom development guarantees blazing-fast load speeds, robust security protocols, and absolute design flexibility. Pre-built templates often bundle heavy dependencies, slow styling frameworks, and security risks, which degrade the premium feel of your brand.

What does 'independent agency' mean for our partnership?

It means direct senior-level focus, transparent communication, and agility. We work without large corporate layers, letting our engineers and designers collaborate directly with your team for speed and precision.

How do you ensure our website is accessible and compliant?

We write semantic HTML5, implement correct ARIA landmarks, optimize keyboard navigation, and target WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines. This ensures your digital products are fully inclusive and performant for everyone.

What is involved in a codebase audit or technical consultancy?

We conduct deep reviews of your existing system's performance, code cleanliness, security gaps, and hosting infrastructure. You receive an actionable roadmap detailing optimizations, security patches, and future-proofing recommendations.
